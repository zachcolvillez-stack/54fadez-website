Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

54FADEZ ("we", "us", "our") operates the website 54fadez.com.au and the barbershop located at 40 Oxleigh Dr, Malaga WA 6090. This policy explains what information we collect through this website, how we use it, and your rights. We are committed to handling your information in line with the Australian Privacy Principles under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth).

What We Collect

When you submit our booking form, we collect only the details you provide:

  • Your first and last name
  • Your phone number
  • Your email address
  • Your requested service, preferred date and time
  • Any notes you choose to add

We do not collect payment details through this website, and we do not use tracking cookies or sell your data to anyone.

How We Use It

The information you submit is used for one purpose only: to contact you and arrange your requested appointment. We may phone, text or email you to confirm, reschedule or follow up on your booking.

How It's Handled

Booking submissions are delivered to us securely through Formspree, a third-party form service, and then sent to our business email. Formspree processes the data only to deliver it to us — you can read their privacy practices at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy. We retain booking enquiries only as long as needed to serve you and keep ordinary business records.

Your Rights

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you at any time. Just get in touch using the details below and we'll action your request.
